Shift in Meaning of “Apology” (R)

ANOVALinear RegressionWelch’s t-Test

Analyzed linguistic evolution of the word “apology” using 5-gram frequency data spanning multiple centuries.

Applied >data cleaning, token pattern extraction, and frequency ratio normalization to quantify yearly semantic proportions.

Used correlation tests, linear regression, Welch’s t-test, ANOVA, and Tukey HSD to statistically validate the historical shift in meaning.
